Our Identity

We are software engineers who pride ourselves on innovation and craftsmanship, and a laser focus on providing you world-class customer service.

Our DNA

We possess several qualities that we value highly when evaluating our own strengths and weaknesses. Here are some things we think you should know about us.

Learners

We are learners. We like learning new things and continuously improving our skills. We enjoy learning about new problems and new ways of seeing things, and challenging the status quo.

  • Our ability to learn quickly keeps us relevant.
  • Our ability to apply knowledge sets us apart.
Listeners

We are listeners. We like hearing what you have to say, and reciving feedback even if it's hard to take. We strive to engage you in every conversation, and show you that you've been heard.

  • Continuous feedback keeps us honest.
  • Continuous dialogue makes us partners.
Leaders

We are leaders. We know the role that servant leadership plays in building great teams, and have the essential ingredients every leader needs: humility, dedication, and a formidable work ethic.

  • Building great teams are what we're all about.
  • Leading them to greatness is our ambition.
Innovators

We are innovators. We approach every problem with ingenuity, creativity, and vision. Singular focus on the problem at hand allows us to come up with truly unique solutions to everyday problems.

  • Creative thinking is our SOP.
  • Breaking new ground is just another day ATO.
Doers

We are doers. We are passionate about executing vision and using our hard-earned talent to build great products. We like to spend hours discussing problems, but our motivation is in solving them.

  • Problems keep us awake at night.
  • Solving them gets us out of bed in the morning.
Builders

We are builders. We like creating things from scratch and experimenting with new ideas to create amazing experiences for end users. We like solving hard problems, and being asked to rise to the challenge.

  • Creativity is our favorite process.
  • Harnessing it is our competitive advantage.

Our Leadership Team

Our leadership team is a husband / wife team with the vision of creating an amazing company in a challenging industry. With an incredibly diverse set of talents ranging from musical excellence to strong leadership, there isn't anything they can't do.
